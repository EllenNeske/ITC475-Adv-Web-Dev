Rules

The rules of this game are simple. Try to get Flappy Cat as far as she can go through the obstacles without hitting anything. Click the "Float up, Kitty Cat!" button to make Flappy Cat go up to avoid the teal bars. If you hit something, it's game over. Hit the refresh button at the top of your brower to keep playing! To see your score, go to the "Scores" section.
